My 2025 with ChatGPT

A year of conversations, code, and collaboration with AI.

I created my OpenAI account on November 13, 2022 — weeks before ChatGPT even launched on November 30th. I was already exploring GPT-3 and the API when ChatGPT arrived and changed everything. What started as early experimentation evolved into a daily collaboration that fundamentally changed how I build, think, and create.

Here's my 2025 wrapped — a look back at how AI became my thinking partner.
